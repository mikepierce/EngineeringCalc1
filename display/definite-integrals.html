Definite Integrals

  1. (Stewart) Prove that \[ \frac{1}{17} \leq \int\limits_1^2 \frac{1}{1+x^4} \,\mathrm{d}x \leq \frac{7}{24} \]
  2. (Stewart) Prove that if \(f\) is continuous, then \[ \int\limits_0^x f(u)(x-u) \,\mathrm{d}u = \int\limits_0^x \left( \int\limits_0^u f(t) \,\mathrm{d}t \right) \,\mathrm{d}x \]
