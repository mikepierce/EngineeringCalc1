Graphing with Calculus

  1. Given this following information about \(f,\) \(f',\) and \(f'',\) graph \(y = f(x)\).

Challenges

  1. Stewart

    Find two points on the curve \(y = x^4 - 2x^2 - x\) that have a common tangent line.

  2. Stewart

    Find a function \(f\) such that \(f'(-1) = \frac 1 2 \), \(f'(0) = 0\), and \(f''(x) \gt 0\) for all \(x\), or prove that such a function cannot exist.
