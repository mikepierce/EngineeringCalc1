Substitution

  1. Evaluate the following integrals.
    1. \(\displaystyle\int \frac 1 2 \csc^2(3x) \,\mathrm{d}x\)
    2. \(\displaystyle\int \tan\left(\pi x-1\right)\sec\left(\pi x-1\right) \,\mathrm{d}x\)
    3. \(\displaystyle\int 3x^3\cos\left( 5x^4\right) \,\mathrm{d}x\)
    4. \(\displaystyle\int x\sec^2\left( 3x^2-9 \right) \,\mathrm{d}x\)
    5. \(\displaystyle\int \sqrt{\tan(x)\sec^4(x)} \,\mathrm{d}x\)
    6. \(\displaystyle\int \frac{\sin\left(\frac{1}{x^2}\right)}{x^3} \,\mathrm{d}x\)
